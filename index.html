The Science of Focus

  • Reduces Procrastination: The 25-minute timer creates a sense of urgency, making it easier to start.
  • Prevents Burnout: Short, scheduled breaks allow the brain to rest, stay fresh, and consolidate information.
  • Improves Planning: Forces you to break down large tasks into smaller, manageable "chunks."
  • Boosts Motivation: Each completed session provides a small, tangible sense of accomplishment.

What is Focus Tree?

Focus Tree is a beautiful, gamified focus timer designed to boost your productivity.

It uses the Pomodoro Technique, combined with tracking and motivational features, to help you build a consistent work habit.

The Focus Timer

  • Set focus durations (5, 10, 15, 20, 25 min)
  • Create custom timers (from 1 to 180 minutes)
  • Take quick 5, 10, or 15 minute breaks
  • Get a "Daily Inspiration" to get started

Personalize Your Focus

Choose ambient sounds and match your mood with beautiful seasonal themes.

Set Daily Goals

  • Define your "Today's Goals" for success
  • Set a target for total sessions
  • Set a target for total minutes
  • Watch your progress bars fill up in real-time

Session History

  • Review a detailed log of every session
  • See total sessions and minutes at a glance
  • Track date, time, and duration
  • Export your focus history

Detailed Statistics

  • View your "Last 7 Days" activity
  • Analyze session durations with a donut chart
  • Track your daily streak for consistency
  • Monitor your average session duration

Gamified Motivation

  • Unlock 8 unique achievements
  • Progress from "First Steps" to "Legend"
  • Get rewarded for sessions and total minutes
  • A fun way to stay consistent!

Technology Behind the Tree

  • React.js: For a dynamic and interactive user interface.
  • JavaScript (ES6+): The core language for all application logic.
  • Tailwind CSS: For the beautiful, responsive, and modern design.
  • HTML5 & CSS3: The foundation of the web application.
  • Vercel: For seamless, high-performance deployment.
